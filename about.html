Nice to meet you! I am a graphic designer studying at Algonquin College who specializes in branding and illustration. I especially enjoy creating logos. I’m a team player, too. It’s surprising just how much my time working as an educator has prepared me for a career in design. Strong communication skills and the ability to adapt to whatever comes your way make all the difference, whether you’re working with a design team or a gaggle of kindergarteners armed with finger paint.
