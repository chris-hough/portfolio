Aries Dog Grooming

Logo and brand design created for Aries Dog Grooming in Petrolia, Ontario.

This brand identity was completed in Autumn of 2016. The process began with a logo design that would be both versatile, and appropriately represent the nature of the business.

A branding guide was provided alongside the logo design. Photography, social media and a range of printed formats were all taken into consideration.
