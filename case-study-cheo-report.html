CHEO annual report

This project started with a rigorous research process to ensure that CHEO's principles were authentically represented with this report. A moodboard and numerous layout sketches established a theme centred on CHEO's goal of letting kids focus on being kids, and not the ailment that led them to the hospital in the first place.

All illustrations were made for this report, and all photography is sourced from stock sites. A calming pastel green was chosen to both signify medical scrubs, and growth and vitality of youth.
