Personal Logo

The goal with my personal logo was to create something vibrant and friendly, that would also be versatile enough to work in almost any scenario.

Since the logo is a fairly simple shape, it can be used very effectively to mask images. Regardless of the tone of a branded piece, the logo can be used in a seamless way.
