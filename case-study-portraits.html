portraits

Portraits created digitally and traditionally as a way of learning new techniques and software. The subjects are composites of stock photography and live models.

These portraits were completed independently. I have always enjoyed portraiture, and use it as a method for experimenting with new techniques, everything from a new marker to a new Illustrator tool.

While I do occasionally strive to accurately depict the model, I often prefer to manipulate the face, either for aesthetic reasons, or to test my understanding of the underlying anatomy.
