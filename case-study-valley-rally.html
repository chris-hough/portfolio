Valley Rally Enduro

Valley Rally Enduro is a new business in the Ottawa region that was looking for a website and brand identity to launch their first official season. Working as a part of a five person team, I took the lead on the logo design.

The client was looking for high energy logo that would work equally well on a website or printed large on a shirt. It had to reflect the industry, without relying on design elements over-used within it.
